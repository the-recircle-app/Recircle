Register ReCircle on VeBetterDAO - Manual Methods

Goal: Get your unique APP_ID for ReCircle by calling the registerApp function

Method 1: VeWorld Console (Recommended)

Step 1: Open VeWorld extension in your browser
Step 2: Make sure you're on VeChain Testnet
Step 3: Look for "DApp Browser" or "Contract" section in VeWorld
Step 4: If available, enter contract: 0xB54f3b684135cC6b54B9C2591c6F33e12f1F0070
Step 5: Call function registerApp with:
• name: ReCircle
• description: Sustainability rewards platform for validating thrift, EV, and rideshare receipts

Method 2: Use MyVeTools.com

Step 1: Go to myvetools.com
Step 2: Look for "Contract Interaction" or "Send Transaction" tool
Step 3: Connect your VeWorld wallet
Step 4: Enter contract address and function details

Method 3: VisualVeChain.com

Step 1: Go to visualvechain.com
Step 2: Switch to testnet mode
Step 3: Use contract interaction tools

Method 4: Raw Transaction Data

After Successful Registration:

  1. Check your transaction on VeChain testnet explorer
