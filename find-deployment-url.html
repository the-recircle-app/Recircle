Find Your ReCircle Deployment URL

Step 1: Find Your Replit URL

Your actual deployment URL should look like one of these:

Format: https://[project-name].replit.app
Example: https://recircle-rewards.replit.app

Look in your Replit browser tab or project settings to find the exact URL.

Step 2: Test Wallet Connection

Once you find your URL, test these paths:

Simple Test: [your-url]/veworld-test
Debug Page: [your-url]/wallet-debug
Full Wallet: [your-url]/dao-wallet-connect

Step 3: Use VeWorld Browser

  1. Open VeWorld app on your device
  2. Go to the in-app browser
  3. Enter your actual Replit URL (not recirclerewards.app)
  4. Make sure VeChain testnet is selected
  5. Navigate to /veworld-test
